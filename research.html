🔬 Research

My research lies at the intersection of machine learning, mathematics, and scientific computing. I am particularly interested in developing models that are not only accurate but also reliable, interpretable, and capable of expressing uncertainty when appropriate.

🚀 Current Research

I am currently working on Uncertainty Quantification for Seismic Velocity Estimation using Flow Matching Models. The goal is to build AI systems that can estimate both predictions and their confidence, making them more useful for scientific and real-world decision making.

"Sometimes the most intelligent prediction is: I’m not completely sure."
